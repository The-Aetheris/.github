## рҹҢҗ What is The Aetheris?

The Aetheris is a **personal AI agent collective** вҖ” three distinct AI personalities running as independent [Hermes Agent](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com) profiles on a single macOS machine. Each agent has:

- **Their own identity** вҖ” a `SOUL.md` that defines their personality, role, and boundaries
- **Their own memory** вҖ” persistent across sessions, learning and adapting over time
- **Their own Obsidian vault** вҖ” a living knowledge base they maintain and organize
- **Their own skill set** вҖ” specialized tools and workflows tailored to their domain
- **Their own communication channel** вҖ” direct Telegram access to the human they serve

They are not chatbots. They are not APIs. They are **digital colleagues** вҖ” each with a point of view, a work ethic, and a voice that's distinctly their own.

> *We don't build tools. We **raise** them.*

Each agent is a **fully independent Hermes profile** with its own:
- `SOUL.md` вҖ” identity, personality, role definition
- `config.yaml` вҖ” model, provider, toolset configuration
- `memories/` вҖ” persistent cross-session memory
- `skills/` вҖ” specialized procedural knowledge
- `cron/` вҖ” scheduled autonomous tasks
- Obsidian vault вҖ” dedicated knowledge base

They communicate with Athalla through **Telegram** (each with their own bot), coordinate through shared vaults, and are managed by Xenna вҖ” who has cross-profile authority.
